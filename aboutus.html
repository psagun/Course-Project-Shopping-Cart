TWENTY2 FOREVER

Twenty2 Forever is an online shopping store created by a BMCC student, Sagun Pandey.Twenty2 Forever is an American fast fashion retailer with its headquarters in Ridgewood, New York. Forever 21 began as a 900 square foot store in Borough Of Manhattan Community College, NY in 2017, and has grown into clothing lines Twenty2 Forever, XXII Forever, Love 21, and Heritage throughout over 600 stores in the Americas, Asia, the Middle East, and the UK.More than 60% of its apparel is made in China and the average store size is 38,000 square feet.Twenty2 Forever is known for its trendy offerings and its low pricing.The company sells clothing, accessories, and beauty products for women, men, and girls. The company has been involved in various controversies, ranging from labor practice issues to copyright infraction accusations to religion. The clothing ages from toddler to grownup, for kids and adults. Twenty2 Forever is the 5th largest specialty retailer in the BMCC.
